# Privacy Policy — Microsoft Fast Login

_Last updated: February 2026_

## What data is collected

Microsoft Fast Login stores the following on your local device only:

- **Email address** — your Microsoft login email
- **Password** — your Microsoft account password
- **Settings** — automation toggle states, URL patterns, and display preferences

## How data is stored

All data is stored exclusively via `chrome.storage.local`, which is:

- Isolated to this extension
- Never accessible by web pages or other extensions
- Never transmitted to any external server or third party

## What data is NOT collected

- No browsing history
- No keystrokes or mouse activity outside of the configured login pages
- No personal communications
- No financial information
- No location data

## Third-party sharing

No user data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party under any circumstances.

## Your control

You can delete all stored data at any time by:
1. Opening the extension popup
2. Clearing your credentials
3. Saving, or uninstalling the extension entirely
